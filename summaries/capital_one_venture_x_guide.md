# Capital One Venture X - Complete Guide

## Card Basics

**Annual Fee:** $395

**Earning Rate:** 2x Capital One miles on everything (uncapped)

**Effective Annual Fee:** Close to $0 or negative after credits

## Sign-Up Bonus

- Competitive sign-up bonuses historically offered
- Check current offers directly with Capital One

## Annual Credits & Benefits

### Travel Portal Credit
- **$300 annual travel credit** (through Capital One Travel portal)
- Can be used for flights, hotels, rental cars

### Anniversary Bonus
- **10,000 anniversary miles** each year (~$100 value)
- Received on card anniversary

### Effective Cost
With $300 travel credit + 10,000 miles ($100 value) = **$400 in annual value**
- Makes the $395 annual fee essentially free or even a small profit

## Lounge Access Benefits

### ⚠️ MAJOR CHANGES (Effective February 1, 2026)

#### Primary Cardholder Access
- Unlimited access to:
  - Priority Pass lounges worldwide
  - Capital One Lounges (exclusive locations like DFW)
  - Plaza Premium lounges

#### Guest Access (NEW RESTRICTIONS)
**Standard Access:**
- **$35 per guest** at Priority Pass lounges
- **$45 per adult guest** at Capital One Lounges
- **$25 for guests ages 2-17** at Capital One Lounges
- **FREE for children under 2**

**Spending Threshold Benefit:**
- Spend **$75,000** annually on the card to unlock:
  - 2 complimentary guests at Capital One Lounges
  - 1 complimentary guest at Capital One Landings

#### Authorized Users
- **$125/year per authorized user** for lounge access
- No more complimentary authorized user lounge access

### Capital One Exclusive Lounges
- Premium lounges at select airports (e.g., DFW)
- Superior experience to standard Priority Pass lounges

## Redemption Strategies

### Best Redemption Options
✓ **Travel Eraser Feature:** 1 cent per mile on any travel purchase
✓ **Transfer to partners:** Variable value, often 1-2+ cents per mile
✓ **Book through Capital One Travel:** Redeem at 1 cent per mile

### AVOID These Redemptions
❌ **Cash back:** Only 0.5 cents per mile (terrible value!)
❌ **Statement credit (non-travel):** Limited value
❌ **Gift cards:** Usually poor redemption rates

### Pro Tips
- Can transfer miles between Capital One accounts
- Convert cash back from Savor One or other Capital One cards to miles
- Combine with no-annual-fee Venture card for flexibility

## Capital One Approval Quirks

### The 3-Bureau Pull
- Capital One pulls **ALL THREE credit bureaus** (Experian, Equifax, TransUnion)
- Results in 3 hard inquiries per application
- **Workaround:** Freeze Experian to reduce to 2 pulls (YMMV)

### Mysterious Approval Algorithm
- No clear approval criteria
- Some people with excellent credit get denied
- May be related to profitability calculations
- **NO reconsideration process** available

### Approval Tips
- Have existing Capital One relationship (checking/savings)
- Show spending on existing Capital One cards
- Be patient with applications
- No guaranteed approval method
