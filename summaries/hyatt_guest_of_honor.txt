HYATT GUEST OF HONOR PROGRAM - COMPLETE GUIDE

================================================================================
WHAT IT IS
================================================================================
A Globalist-exclusive benefit that lets you book award stays for other people,
and they receive your full Globalist benefits during their stay.

================================================================================
HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================
- Book using your Hyatt points for someone else
- Reservation goes under their name, not yours
- Book via phone/chat with Hyatt customer service
- No official limit on number of rooms per booking

================================================================================
ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================
- ONLY for Globalist members (60+ nights/year or via credit card status)
- Guest doesn't need any Hyatt status

================================================================================
BENEFITS THE GUEST RECEIVES
================================================================================
- Suite upgrades (when available)
- Free breakfast for 2 adults + 2 children
- 4 PM late checkout
- Waived resort fees
- Club lounge access
- Free parking on award stays

================================================================================
IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS
================================================================================
- Award stays only (NOT cash bookings)
- Must book through Hyatt (can't do it online yourself)
- NEW RULE: 10 award limit - guests can only receive 10 Guest of Honor stays
  per year (to prevent abuse/reselling)
- The guest earns the elite night credits, NOT you

================================================================================
PRO TIPS & STRATEGIES
================================================================================
- Perfect for family trips or group travel
- Can book multiple rooms at once
- Great for special occasions
- Example: Booking 5 rooms for girlfriend's family (10 people) at Leela
  Mangus Bali with full Globalist perks

================================================================================
WHY IT'S SPECIAL
================================================================================
No other major hotel program lets you share top-tier status benefits this
comprehensively with non-members.

================================================================================
RECENT UPDATES
================================================================================
- Hyatt made it easier to gift awards online
- New restriction of 10 received awards per year implemented to prevent
  black market sales
- Guest earns elite night credits from the stay
